ABOUT VIRGINIA

Virginia is an artist who loves the beauty of the digital and natural worlds, and their intersections.

In her explorations of different mediums (such as with colored paper or mirror tiles or paint) and subjects (such as butterflies, birds, trees) she hopes to prove that pixels are more than just a product of the digital age. That pixels are a building block of life.

Virginia currently works full time as a web developer in Washington D.C. and is open to projects in both the web and art spaces.
